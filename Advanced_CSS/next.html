TN Creek Fish Species

When creek fishing, you can expect a variety of species depending on location, but I'll give a quick run-down of species I've caught locally.

Bluegill/Sunfish

I've caught all manner of bluegills, green sunfish, redbreast sunfish, warmouth, and hybrids.

Rock Bass

Rock bass are quite common as well.

Largemouth Bass

Occasionally I'll catch a largemouth bass, but ironically never very large.

Creek Chubs/Shiners

One of my most common catches tends to be creek chubs or some sort of shiner. Some creek chubs I've caught have been close to 7 inches in length.
