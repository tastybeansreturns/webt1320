Why Bother Fishing Ponds?

Over the years, I've frequently fished ponds for largemouth bass. Ponds have always been more accessible to me than rivers, and oftentimes less pressured. Ponds can also harbor some chonky bass. Bankfishing ponds is as good as it gets - no boat needed. Even better, I don't need to bring a plethora of baits with me when I fish - I only need 3 kinds (in a few basic color schemes) to catch fish all day.

How Do You Find Ponds?

Google Maps is my best friend when it comes to finding ponds to fish. I'll switch on the satelite setting and just start scrolling around my area. There's probably over 20 ponds within 5 miles of where I live. I'll scan for ponds in neighborhoods and behind businesses. Though most ponds will be readily accessible, some may be private; it's always a good idea to get permission to fish a private or "residents only" pond and have a back-up pond in case you get rejected.
